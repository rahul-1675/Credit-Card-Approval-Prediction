Metrics Key Definitions:
  • Accuracy: Proportion of total correct predictions.
  • Precision: Out of all applications predicted as "Approved", how many were actually correct (minimizes bad loans).
  • Recall: Out of all applications that should be "Approved", how many did the model find (minimizes lost revenue).
  • F1-Score: Harmonic mean of Precision and Recall. Essential for balanced classification.
  • ROC-AUC: Area Under the Receiver Operating Characteristic curve. Measures model discriminative power between classes.
